About

Made for bored students, not rule‑breakers.

This is a personal project: a clean homepage that looks like an unblocked games site but stays focused on chill games and homework help.

Simple idea

Give students something fun to click on during downtime, without tons of ads or sketchy popups.

Study + play

The homework helper panel makes it easier to remember assignments and keep track of what you actually need to finish.

No bypass tools

This site is not made to hack, bypass filters, or mess with school systems—just games and simple study tools.
